Reason behind the name Cepheus:

We name the mission, "Cepheus" after the constellation Cepheus. A constellation shaped like a house. Since the mission is looking for a home outside the Earth, naming the mission "Cepheus" aligns with our goals of leaving Earth and finding a new home. The name choice adds meaning to our mission.
